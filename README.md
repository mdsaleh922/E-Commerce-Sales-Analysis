# Sales Performance Analysis Dashboard

## 📊 Project Overview

    This project consists of an interactive dual-dashboard system designed to monitor retail performance.
    It tracks $32.87M in Net Revenue across various product categories, regions, and timeframes, providing 
    a granular look at profitability and consumer behavior.

## 🚀 Key Metrics (KPIs)
    Net Revenue: $32.87M

    Gross Revenue: $37.913M

    Total Profit: $1.64M

    Avg. Order Value: $657

    Total Orders: 50K

    Avg. Customer Rating: 3.00 / 5.0

    Total Quantity Sold: 150K units

## 📈 Dashboard Features
    1. Sales Performance Dashboard
     Focuses on operational efficiency and categorical breakdown.

    Daily Revenue Trend: Identifies Friday as the peak performance day ($4.88M), showing a significant spike compared to earlier in the week.

    Revenue by Payment Mode: Analyzes customer preferences across Wallet, UPI, COD, Credit, and Debit cards (Wallet leading at $6.7M).

    Regional Performance: A waterfall chart detailing revenue contributions from the Middle East, North America, Asia, and Europe.

    Category Analysis: Donut charts illustrating that Beauty, Books, and Fashion contribute nearly equally to total revenue (~16% each).

    Discount Contribution: Visualizes how different discount tiers impact total sales volume.

    2. Time Series Analysis Dashboard
    Focuses on historical trends and cyclical patterns.

    Monthly Revenue Trend: Tracks fluctuations from January to September, highlighting a significant dip in February ($2.51M) and a recovery peak in August ($2.85M).

    Monthly Profitability: Correlates revenue with profit, showing consistent profit margins across the year despite revenue volatility.

    Weekly Distribution: Breaks down orders, profit, and quantity sold by the day of the week, revealing that weekend sales (Saturday/Sunday) maintain high volume and profit consistency.

## 💡 Key Insights
    The "Friday Effect": Revenue peaks sharply on Fridays, suggesting a prime window for marketing campaigns or flash sales.

    Payment Diversity: Revenue is remarkably balanced across payment methods, indicating a well-integrated payment gateway system.

    Stability in Categories: No single category dominates the market; the business maintains a healthy, diversified portfolio across Electronics, Sports, Beauty, and Fashion.

    Regional Growth: Europe and Asia represent significant revenue pillars in the global distribution.
